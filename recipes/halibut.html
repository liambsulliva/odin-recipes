Halibut with Coconut Curry Broth

  • Back

    • Ingredients

    • 4 x 6oz Skinless Halibut Filets (can sub with Tilapia)
    • 4 Chopped Shallots
    • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
    • 2.5 tsp Curry Paste
    • 2 Cups Chicken Broth
    • 1/2 Coconut Milk
    • 1/2 Cup Chopped Cilantro
    • 2 Chopped Scallions
    • 2 tbsp Lime Juice
    • Salt/Pepper

    Steps

    1. Heat oil in large skillet on medium heat.
    2. Add shallots and cook for 5 minutes or until browned.
    3. Add curry paste and combine. Leave for 30 seconds.
    4. Add chicken broth, coconut milk, and salt. Simmer for 5 minutes or until reduced.
    5. Season Halibut and place into pan. Shake until fish is covered in sauce.
    6. Cook fish until it is opaque and easily flakes. About 7 minutes.
    7. Stir cilantro, scallions, and lime juice into sauce. Pour over fish and serve.
