Salmon Rub

No ingredient amounts on salmon rub (family recipe, sorry)

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Salmon Rub (Turbinado sugar, smoked sea salt, black pepper, coriander, orange zest, celery seed, cumin, fennel, dill, star anise)

Steps

  1. Set oven to 375F
  2. Pour salmon rub ingredients in bowl, mix until incorporated
  3. Take out deep dish pan, coat with olive oil and place fish on tray
  4. Coat salmon in oil and sprinkle a modest amount of rub on the top of the filet(s)
  5. Bake for 20 minutes (amount may fluctuate depending on thickness of fish, use a cooking thermometer)
