High Protein Granola

  • Back

    • Ingredients

    • 2 cups Rolled Oats
    • 1/2 Cup Protein Powder (Vanilla or Plain)
    • 1/3 Cup Peanut Butter
    • 1/4 Cup Honey

    Steps

    1. Preheat Oven to 350F.
    2. Mix oats and protein powder in a bowl.
    3. Microwave peanut butter in spurts until it is melted without burning.
    4. Mix peanut butter with honey.
    5. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix well.
    6. Transfer the mix onto the sheet, spread out into a flat layer.
    7. Bake for 13-15 minutes.
