Pan Fried Veggies

  • Back

    • Ingredients (Rub)

    • Green Veggie (Green Beans, Broccoli, Asparagus, Brussel Sprouts)
    • Olive Oil
    • Soy Sauce
    • Salt/Pepper
    • Aromat

    Steps

    1. Wash veggie of choice and lightly season with aromat, salt, and pepper
    2. Set pan to medium-high heat
    3. Pour approximately 1 tbsp of olive oil, heat until it runs in the pan
    4. Lower heat to medium, toss in veggies and cover pan
    5. Sauté for 2 minutes, then add in a pinch of soy sauce
    6. When the soy sauce starts to burn, drain pan and serve
